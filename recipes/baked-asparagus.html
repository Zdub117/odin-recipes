Baked Asparagus

Description

Delightfully prepared oven roasted asparagus seasoned with garlic, lemon, and parmesan cheese. The perfect side dish to compliment chicken, fish, and lamb.

Ingredients

  • One bunch of asparagus
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1.5 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 0.5 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
