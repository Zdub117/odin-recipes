Breakfast Bowls

Ingredients

  • 2 large russet potatoes
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 0.5 tsp garlic powder
  • 0.25 tsp salt
  • 0.25 tsp ground black pepper
  • 6 large eggs
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 1 cup salsa of your choice
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat over to 400 degrees F (204 degrees C).
  2. Rinse the potatoes in water and cut them into quarter inch cubes.
  3. Place the potatoes into a large mixing bowl. Sprinkle the smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and 1 Tbsp of oil into the bowl. Toss until the potatoes are evenly coated.
  4. Coat a baking sheet with the final 1 Tbsp of olive oil.
  5. Spread the potatoes out evenly onto the baking sheet.
  6. Place the potatoes into the oven for 40 minutes.
  7. Roughly 10 minutes before the potatoes are completed crack all 6 eggs into a large bowl. Gently wisk the eggs, and if desired add a pinch of salt and pepper.
  8. Place a skillet onto the stove and set it to medium heat. Place the butter into the skillet to melt.
  9. Once the butter is melted pour in the wisked eggs. Gently move the eggs around the skillet with a spatalu/turner cooking until mostly solid but soft and glistening. At this point add in the cheese and allow the cheese to melt into the eggs.
  10. Remove the eggs from the skillet and place into a large bowl. The potatoes, now cooked, should be added to the bowl as well. Gently toss the bowl to evenly distribute the potatoes and cheesy eggs.
  11. Split into 4 even bowls. Serve warm and topped with salsa.
